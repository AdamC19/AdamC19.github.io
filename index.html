Welcome to the personal website of Adam Cordingley. On this site, you can find information pertinent to Adam's research at Marshall University, interests, and projects.

Adam is a senior at Huntington High School in Huntington, WV, USA. Adam is interested in engineering, science (particularly physics), robotics, and computing. He has been involved in VEX competitive robotics since 2012 and is cofounder of VRC Team 8768A. Adam also enjoys tinkering with Arduino microcontrollers and C++ programming, playing the clarinet, and tropical fish keeping.
