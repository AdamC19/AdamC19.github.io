--- title: Research ---

Adam has been involved in several research projects at Marshall University (MU). Information pertinent to those projects can be found on this page.

2015

During June and July of 2015, Adam worked as a lab assistant at the Marhsall University Molecular and Biological Imaging Center (MU MBIC) for Dr. Mike Norton. Primarily, Adam worked on imaging Gold Nanoparticles using Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM).

Adam also operated the lab's Leapfrog Creatr 3D printer with moderate success. Adam's thingiverse page can be found here.

2014

During the spring of 2014, Adam conducted research using SEM studying the wear on oboe reeds due to playing over time. This was done in conjunction with the MU MBIC and Dr. Richard Kravchak, oboist and director of the MU School of Music and Theatre. Adam presented a poster on this project at Marshall's Sigma Xi day titled "Wear Analysis of Oboe Reeds with Scanning Electron Microscopy". The poster is displayed below and can also be viewed here.
